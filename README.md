## About me

I'm a software engineer with over **9 years of experience** building modern web applications. I'm based in Montevideo, Uruguay, and I work across the full stack — though I'm most at home on the frontend.

- **Frontend:** I specialize in **React** and **TypeScript**, building scalable, performant UIs that people actually enjoy using.
- **Backend:** I've delivered production solutions with **Python**, **Node.js**, and **C#**.
- **Design:** I'm deeply interested in UI/UX — I care about crafting interfaces that are both visually refined and intuitive to use.
